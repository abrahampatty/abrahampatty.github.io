Q&A

If you have any other questions other than what we’ve listed here, please reach out to us.

Question

When should I RSVP by?

Answer

Kindly RSVP before Aug 1st 2019. It is a sitting-dinner reception and you will be assigned a seat, so we will have to know of your attendance weeks prior to our big day.

Question

Is there a dress code?

Answer

There is no particular dresscode! Please wear whichever clothes you deem appropriate for a wedding! You may note that our party will be held outdoor with a rustic theme.

Question

Can I bring a date?

Answer

Since it is a sitting-dinner reception, we have limited seats. However, you may bring a +1 if you wish, just make sure they are also RSVP-ed when you fill in the RSVP form so we can prepare them a seat! :)

Question

What time should I arrive?

Answer

Please arrive according to the schedule provided. 10.00 AM for the Holy Matrimony and 18.00 for the Reception Dinner.

Question

Are kids welcome?

Answer

We love your kids—we really do. But we want our wedding to be your night off and to make sure you don't worry the whole time watching your kids play outside. It is up to your discretion, whether to bring your kids or not. We believe in our guests' judgement :)
